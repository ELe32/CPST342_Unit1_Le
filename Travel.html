Phu Quoc is an island known for fish sauce manufacturing in the early age, but it has recently become the hottest
travel attraction. Phu Quoc travel rate increases by 25% from the prior year. A short flight from Saigon will get
you there to enjoy scenic beaches and great seafood. Not only will you be amazed by the natural attractions, you
will soon discover many great seafood restaurants.

I came back to visit a few years ago and I hardly recognize all my childhood landmarks as many high-end hotels
and restaurants rose up from every corner. Seeing with my own eyes how an old fishermen village transformed into
a Vietnam main attraction was priceless. I truly believe Phu Quoc has some of the most beautiful beaches in the world.
